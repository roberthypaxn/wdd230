Bountiful Foods

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to publicise and sell organic, locally grown food products. These foods will be cultivated and grown in Carlsbad, California, however Bountiful Foods will sell them around the United States, especially over the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, and even internationally. The website provides information for customers and businesses about available products, product prices, shipment, logistic and transport prices. The site also provides a Fresh to create one's own specialty drink from organic fruits and have it delivered to them, an About us page for informing people about the nature, address and contact of the business. Lastly the site will have an attribution page that references outside content.
