Musanze District, Rwanda

A place of mountains, forestry, and Gorillas

Musanze is the district where Rwanda Volcanoes National Park is located. Virunga chambers offers businesses owners in this area sustainable means to become a self-reliant network of like minded enterpreneurs.
According to the Rwandan government's 2012 census, the population of the area around Volcanoes National Park is approximately 23,000 people.

Something For Everyone

Quality * Beauty * With a Smile

Located on a hillside of Bisate hill, is this beautiful shelter for tourists and hikers named Bisate Lodge.
Hike some of the largest mountains in Africa: Karisimbi and Sabyinyo are 2 of the 5 dormant volcanoes that are enjoyable to hike.
Get proactive with Gorilla tracking in Virunga National Park. The park is home to about 604 mountain gorillas, which is over half of the world's total population of this endangered species.
